Worthly Smarter Decisions, Made Clear Worthly is a utility app that helps users make clearer decisions before purchases or commitments. The app guides users through structured questions and provides an objective verdict based on their own inputs. Key features: • Structured decision guidance • Clear verdict summaries • Optional account for history and cloud sync • Worthly Pro subscription for advanced features Worthly does not provide financial, legal, or professional advice.
